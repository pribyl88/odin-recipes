Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 (scant) cup almond flour
  • 1.5 cups coconut sugar or light brown sugar
  • 0.5 cup granulated sugar
  • 1.5 tsp baking soda
  • 1 packet of vanilla pudding powder
  • 1 tsp flaky sea salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 15 oz milk chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375ÂºF.
  2. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  3. Whisk together all dry ingredients EXCEPT the flaky sea salt.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the wet ingredients.
  5. Stir the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients bit-by-bit until well-mixed.
  6. Add the milk chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop heaping tablespoons of the dough and roll them into balls using your palms.
  8. Arrange your balled-up-dough on your baking sheet, leaving room for expansion.
  9. Bake until edges are golden-brown but centers look slightly under-baked (they'll continue cooking once out of the oven); likely 10-12 minutes.
  10. Remove from oven & sprinkle with flaky salt.
  11. Cool on pan for at least two minutes, then transfer to cooling rack to finish cooling.
  12. Put them in your mouth & chew. We'll stop there but the National Institute of Health would be more than happy to walk you through the rest of the process if curiosity gets the best of you.
